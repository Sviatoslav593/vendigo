FINANCIAL PLANNING

Our core service is financial planning. This process begins with a free consultation with an Vendigo Consulting specialist. During the in-person meeting, he or she will ask you a series of of questions related to your financial situation, habits and goals, and your answers will become the foundation upon which we will build your financial plan.

The company employs young and ambitious experts united by their common vision of the financial world and its role in the lives of each of us. We believe that finance should be at the service of people's life goals and lead to positive outcomes. for everyone, not just those who understand how to benefit from them.

SEE STEPS
1. COMPLETING A FINANCIAL PROFILE
The purpose of the meeting is to establish your financial situation and goals. Topics, you will discuss with your advisor are primarily your income and expenses, and your use of important financial products such as mortgages, deposits and insurance. All of this shapes your financial profile.
2. CREATING A FINANCIAL PLAN
Based on the information you provide, we will define your financial plan. The financial plan includes guidelines and specific steps, to take to budget, save, spend and manage your personal finances more effectively. This may include advice on the most beneficial insurance plans for you, the most sensible way to take care of your savings, how to diversify your investments, how to achieve financial security with the and other useful recommendations.
3. ASSISTANCE IN THE IMPLEMENTATION OF THE FINANCIAL PLAN
When you are ready to proceed with the financial plan, we will assist you in purchasing, activating and managing the products and services needed to implement it. We will save you time and nerves by helping you find the best and negotiating with financial institutions.
Financial Planning by Vendigo Consulting

Meetings with clients are held at their convenience and place. Our consultants are ready to meet with You after business hours, at your home or office. If prefer to meet at our office, it is located in a convenient and communicative location, right by the exit of Opalchenska metro station in Sofia.

Vendigo Consulting advisors are your partners, and your financial situation is their concern. As the company is an independent advisor and does not offer its own products on the market, you can rest assured, that you will never be advised to purchase a financial product that is not to your advantage.

Vendigo Consulting does not charge fees or commissions to its clients. The company profits from contracts with its partners.
